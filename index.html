About us

Swebs Careers is a human resource consulting firm that provides recruitment services, career and leadership development services for businesses across various sectors within Africa.

Our goal is to provide clients with top, quality talents that meet each organization's needs. We are known as a frontlier in meeting and exceeding our client's expectations.
